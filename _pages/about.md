I am a Master's student in Computational Linguistics at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München.

My research focuses on large language models, reasoning, evaluation, and retrieval-augmented generation. I am particularly interested in how reasoning traces can be evaluated, transferred, and interpreted across models.

Currently, I am working on cross-model reasoning transfer, studying how chain-of-thought traces produced by one model can be used by other models under different trace-use modes.
